## Store listing

### Short description

Access Dclub offers, promotions, and mobile membership services.

### Full description

Dclub helps users access club offers, promotions, and membership-related mobile services in one Android app.

With Dclub, users can:

- Open the Dclub mobile service journey quickly
- Access promotional and campaign pages on Android
- Complete mobile number and OTP verification flows
- Use a simple full-screen experience designed for mobile devices

The app is built to provide a fast and direct way to open Dclub web-based services from Android while keeping the experience easy to use.
